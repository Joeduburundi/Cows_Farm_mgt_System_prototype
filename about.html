About Us

About Our History


Ankoe Farm began in December 1989 as His Excellece's project( President Yoweri Kaguta Museveni) to teach members of the westeren region that is Kiruhura,Lyantonde and all neighbours the modern farming activities.
Currently our farm is a model one in the region and is also a research center for undergraduate students pursuing veterinary studies.

Our Mission


To provide the best Quality products that can meet the consumers' needs without harm and also meeet the world market.

Our Vision


Providing a suitable site for our animals and protect them from all dangers of torture and illness plus having responsible skilled veterinary officers and other managers to manage farm activites.
